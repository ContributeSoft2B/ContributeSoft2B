Frequently Asked Questions


What is Soft2B?

Soft2B Chain (STB Chain) is an ecosystem application system, with its native blockchain (STB Chain), for the software industry based on blockchain technology. The main objective of the Soft2B platform (STB Platform) is to establish a secure, stable and trustworthy platform for software developers and users to efficiently carry out various basic functions in the distribution of software (i.e. protect software copyright, authorize transactions, grant permissions, carry out transfers and verification).

What is STB?

STB is the short name of Soft2B's original token. Tokens are generated once in Soft2B's creation zone and used as a circulating token in the Soft2B application layer for asset transactions. They can also be used as witness fees paid to witnesses in the payment process.

The distribution of STB token?

The total amount of STB token is 300 million and is distributed as follows:
65% - 195 million distributed by market Approximately 5% will be allocated to the angel stage. About 20% will be allocated to the private placement stage which will be open to specific groups only. 5% - 15 million distributed to the founding team The lock-up period is 3 years.
15% - 45 million reserved by foundation Used as the fee of the normal operation of the SOFT2B Foundation to support the growth of participating companies across the entire web platform enabling SOFT2B technology to be rapidly deployed in physical enterprises.
15% - 45 million community support and marketing Used to reward outstanding code contributors within the community,promote community and provide ecosystem services, etc.

What advantages or highlights does STB have?

The core goal of Soft2B is to establish a decentralized software and reliable specification of a credible ecosystem. Connecting each software developer, consumers can trace the source channels, software information and time and so on of each device software. The developer of software can also track the service and management by using the electronic proofing technology. STB use blockchain technology to confirm the software, make the software more explicit, and realize the software asset. When the software users do not want to use the software, they can sell the serial number of the software through the wallet and realize the reuse of the software.
